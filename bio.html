ELADIO CARRION'S BIO

Eladio Carrión is a Latin GRAMMY-winning, multi-platinum rapper and singer-songwriter who has established himself as one of the most captivating musical acts in Spanish Trap and Reggaeton. Carrión has made an impressive impact with 5 albums that have reached the top 10 on Billboard’s “Top Latin Albums.” He is internationally acclaimed for his stellar improvisations and freestyle skills, which demonstrate his noteworthy creative versatility. His music videos have more than 5 billion views on YouTube, while his music has amassed more than 5.1 billion streams across digital streaming platforms. Consistently at the helm of the charts, Eladio has collaborated with the likes of 50 Cent, Bad Bunny, Daddy Yankee, Future, Karol G, Lil Wayne and Quavo, among many others.


His album, “3MEN2 KBRN,” reached #4 on Apple Music’s “Top Albums” and #2 on “Top Latin Albums.” On Spotify it debuted at #1 on “Top Albums Debut USA” and “Top Albums Debut Global.” “3MEN2 KBRN” was also named among the best albums of 2023 by Billboard, Complex and Rolling Stone. On top of the album milestones, “Coco Chanel” notched the star his first Billboard Hot 100 entry, earned him his first Latin GRAMMY for “Best Rap/Hip-Hop Song” and was featured in Rolling Stone’s “The 100 Best Songs of 2023.” In 2023, Eladio took his “The Sauce Tour” across the United States, Latin America, and Europe, while also appearing at coveted events like Coachella, Governors Ball, SXSW, and the Billboard Latin Music Awards.


His latest album, "Sol María," serves as a heartfelt tribute to his mother and landed on the Billboard 200, Top Latin Albums and Latin Rhythm Albums at #6 and #3 respectively. The album also soared to #1 on Apple Music's Top Latin Albums chart, #2 on the Top Albums chart, and #1 on Spotify’s Top Albums Debut Global. To amplify this standout album release, Eladio dazzled with his debut late night appearance on The Tonight Show Starring Jimmy Fallon before announcing and selling out three consecutive shows at the Coliseo de Puerto Rico. Currently, Eladio is amping up to bring the magic of Sol María to Spain, promising fans an unforgettable experience, while also collaborating on a comedy series, Rubicon Global Media, reflecting on his life as a Puerto Rican-American teenager. Carrión's multifaceted talent and global impact continue to captivate audiences worldwide.
